    Reports

    The following are available reports on content, user-adoption, and various aspects of the system

    Content-related Reports

    Notifications
    Information about our Notification content, including aggregates for content status and a report on description/notification text lengths.
    Triggers
    A breakdown of all the types of triggers in use in the system. This includes users' custom triggers as well as the default triggers associated with notifications.
    Authors
    This report is an initial attempt at author productivity; At the moment, it simply counts the items owned by different people that are in various states (draft, published, etc).

    User-interaction Reports

    Popular Content selected by Organization Members
    This report lists the most popular public goals selected by organization members.
    Notification Engagement
    This report includes information about how users are engaging with the app's push notifications (actions & custom actions within the app).
    User Report
    This report includes information user signups, daily usage, and some minimal demographic information.
    Popular Content
    This report includes the most popular types of content. That is, the most-selected items in the public library of content within the app.
